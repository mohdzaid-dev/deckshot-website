Overview & Scope

DeckShot is a developer interview preparation application available on the Google Play Store (Android). This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • The DeckShot mobile application (Android)
  • The DeckShot website at deckshot.in
  • All services, features, and content offered under the DeckShot brand

This policy is compliant with the Information Technology Act, 2000, the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011, and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) of India.

Plain language summary: We collect only what we need to run the service. We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. Your payment information is processed by Razorpay — we never see or store your card details.

Payment Data & Processing

DeckShot uses Razorpay Software Private Limited ("Razorpay") as its payment gateway for processing all in-app purchases. Razorpay is a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor regulated by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI).

4.1 What Razorpay Collects

When you make a payment, Razorpay collects and processes your payment instrument details (card number, UPI ID, net banking credentials, wallet details). DeckShot does not receive, store, or have access to your full card number, CVV, PIN, or banking passwords at any point.

4.2 What DeckShot Receives

  • Razorpay Order ID and Payment ID
  • Payment status (success / failure / pending)
  • Amount paid and currency
  • Timestamp of transaction
  • Masked payment method (e.g., "card ending in 4242" or "UPI")

4.3 Payment Verification

All payment verifications are performed server-side via Razorpay webhooks using cryptographic signature verification. Client-side payment confirmation is never trusted as the sole source of truth.

4.4 Refund Policy

Refunds are processed at our discretion. If you believe you were incorrectly charged, contact us at contact@deckshot.in within 7 days of purchase. Verified erroneous charges will be refunded to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

4.5 Transaction Records

Transaction records are retained for a minimum of 7 years as required under Indian accounting and tax regulations (Income Tax Act, 1961 and GST laws).

Razorpay Privacy Policy: Payments are governed by Razorpay's own privacy policy and terms of service, available at razorpay.com/privacy. DeckShot is not responsible for Razorpay's data practices.

Data Storage & Security

6.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

Your account data, progress, bookmarks, and purchase records are stored on Supabase servers located in the ap-southeast-1 region (Singapore). This data is secured by:

  • Row Level Security (RLS) policies enforced at the database level — each user can only access their own data
  • TLS/SSL encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Supabase's SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure

6.2 Security Measures We Implement

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt — never stored in plain text
  • Authentication tokens are short-lived and rotate automatically
  • Payment webhook signatures are cryptographically verified server-side
  • Offline question data on your device is encrypted with AES-256-CBC with PBKDF2 key derivation (5,000 iterations)
  • No sensitive data is stored in unencrypted local storage

6.3 Security Limitations

No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. While we implement industry-standard security measures, we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights and freedoms, we will notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, as required by applicable law.

07 /

Local & Offline Storage

DeckShot allows you to download question decks for offline use. The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Downloaded question decks: Stored in AES-256-CBC encrypted format in AsyncStorage. The encryption key is derived using PBKDF2 with a device-specific salt.
  • Session tokens: Authentication credentials stored in the device's secure local storage.
  • App preferences: Theme settings, notification preferences, and last-viewed position — stored in non-sensitive local storage.

Local data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers except when syncing your progress. You can delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the app. Reinstalling will require re-downloading any previously downloaded decks.

Your Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and applicable Indian law, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

8.1 Right to Access

You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you, including the categories of data, the purposes for which it is processed, and details of any sharing with third parties.

8.2 Right to Correction

You have the right to request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold about you. You can update your name and email directly within the app's Profile section.

8.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data. Upon request, we will delete your account and associated personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g., financial transaction records).

8.4 Right to Data Portability

You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format (JSON/CSV) and to transmit it to another controller.

8.5 Right to Withdraw Consent

Where processing is based on your consent, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal.

8.6 Right to Nominate

Under the DPDP Act, 2023, you have the right to nominate another individual to exercise your data rights in the event of your death or incapacity.

8.7 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of the above rights, email us at contact@deckshot.in with the subject line "Data Rights Request". We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

Cookies & Tracking

11.1 Mobile App

The DeckShot mobile app does not use browser cookies. The app uses the following local storage mechanisms:

  • AsyncStorage: For storing authentication tokens, user preferences, and encrypted offline content

Our website may use the following types of cookies:

  • Strictly necessary cookies: Required for the website to function. Cannot be disabled.

11.3 Opt-Out of Analytics

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or by enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser settings.
