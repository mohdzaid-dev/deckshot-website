// Questions

Everything you want to know

Is the app free to download?
Yes. Download is completely free. You get 10–15 free questions per topic to experience the format. After that, you can unlock the full deck for a one-time payment.
Is it really a one-time payment?
Absolutely. Pay once per topic and it's yours forever. No monthly subscription, no renewal, no hidden charges. We mean it when we say lifetime access.
What if I'm a fresher with no experience?
DeckShot is perfect for freshers. Questions are categorised by difficulty — start from Easy, build your confidence, then move to Medium and Hard. The interview-style answers teach you how to communicate concepts clearly even if you're just learning them.
Will this work offline?
Yes. Download any topic deck while on WiFi and study without internet. Metro ride, flight, anywhere. Your progress and bookmarks are saved too.
How are the questions curated?
Every question is sourced from real interviews reported by candidates at Indian and global product companies. Filtered for frequency — only questions that appear repeatedly make the cut.
What companies are the questions from?
Flipkart, Zomato, Swiggy, Razorpay, Google, Amazon, Meta, Microsoft, Meesho, Paytm, ShareChat, Ola, and more. Each card is tagged with the companies that asked it.
Is iOS available?
Android is live on the Play Store now. iOS is coming soon. Drop your email at contact@deckshot.in and we'll notify you when it launches.
How do I get support?
Email us at contact@deckshot.in. We typically respond within 24 hours. You can also report any incorrect answer directly from within the app using the Report button on any card.
